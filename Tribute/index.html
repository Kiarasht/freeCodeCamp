Dr. Neil Tyson

Astrophysicist & Cosmologist


Dr. Neil showing a vulcan salute.


Dr. Neil's time line from his birth to now:

  • 1958: Born in Manhattan, New York City.
  • 1967: Gained an interest in astronomy.
  • 1980: Bachelor's degree in physics from Harvard University.
  • 1983: Master's degree in astronomy from University of Texas.
  • 1989: Master's in astrophysics from Columbia University.
  • 1991: Doctorate in astrophysics from Columbia University.
  • 1991: Joined the Hayden Planetarium as a staff scientist.
  • 1995: Wrote monthly essays for Natural History magazine, some of which were collected in Death by Black Hole: And Other Cosmic Quandaries
  • 2004: Host of the four-episode television series Origins,
  • 2006: Host of the television series NOVA scienceNOW
  • 2014: Hosted the television series Cosmos: A Spacetime Odyssey.
